Scroll down to meet the two main characters of this story: Onat and Eda.

EDA

Born in 2000, Eda is a chemoinformatics drug designer, currently based in Paris — though God knows where she will end up next. She has a talent for arranging, organizing, and making things feel just right.

She loves cooking, reading, playing video games, and almost every type of exercise. Lately, swimming has become her new favorite activity. She also loves spending time in nature, going on picnics, practicing yoga from time to time, and painting with watercolors.

Eda prefers cozy gatherings over loud clubs and is usually known as the "mom friend" of the group — the one who makes sure everyone is okay, fed, and emotionally supported.

ONAT

Born in 2001, Onat is the goofy sidekick of this story — and yes, younger than Eda. He is a graphic designer with a very specific meme taste that Eda still cannot fully understand.

He loves trying new things, although he rarely does the same thing twice, even when he actually liked it. In many ways, he is the complete opposite of Eda. He is more introverted, not always easy to reach by phone — we apologize for that, but he is getting better, and he truly does not do it intentionally.

Even though he enjoys his quiet space, with Eda he is always ready to join the adventure, wherever it takes them: meeting new people, visiting museums, going out, or even going to the gym — sometimes with a little bit of force.

And when his introvert energy finally takes over, they both switch to cozy mode: staying home, avoiding the world, and having the best time together.
